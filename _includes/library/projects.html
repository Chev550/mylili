Performance evaluation of building integrated solar thermal shading system

Joint work with Ming Qu and Steve Peng West Lafayette

This research evaluated the performance of the building integrated solar thermal shading system in terms of energy saving, daylight provision, and solar usage based on a medium office building in Los Angeles.
